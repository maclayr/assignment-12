This map shows apartments, grocery stores, coffee shops, banks, and gas stations located in the South End neighborhood of Charlotte, North Carolina. All locations are near the "Rail Trail." The Rail Trail is a multiuse path that runs along the Light Rail track in the area. The Light Rail is the local public transprotation system. The noted locations on this map are included to help someone who may be looking to move to the South End neghborhood of Charlotte, North Carolina.
